# Final-Year-Project
The AI-Based Food Nutrition Estimator for Pakistani Mess Food is an innovative final year project developed at The Islamia University of Bahawalpur, Department of Information Technology. This project addresses a significant and largely unresolved public health challenge: the complete absence of nutritional awareness tools tailored for Pakistani hostel and university canteen food environments. University students residing in mess facilities across Pakistan consume daily meals that include traditional dishes such as daal, chawal, aloo gosht, biryani, roti, and various regional curries, without any knowledge of the caloric content or macronutrient composition of what they eat.
